Glossary
++++++++

.. glossary:: 

    Show
      A show is a group of season and episodes.

    Season
      A season is a full set of sequential episodes of a show.

    Episode
      An episode is a sequence of scenes and shots.
      
    Scene
      A scene is a sequence of sequential shots.

    Shot
      A shot is animated content generated by a single file from an animation cretion tool.

    Administrator
      The higest level of user.  Can do anything any of the other user levels can do plus more

    Supervisor
      Supervisors can assign and evaluate tasks.

    Members
      Members are artists that perform assigned tasks.
